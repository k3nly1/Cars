Popular Car Brands

A short list of popular brands: Toyota, BMW, Mercedes, Ford, Honda, and others.

The automotive industry has produced many legendary brands over the decades. Each brand has its own unique history, philosophy, and approach to engineering. From luxury vehicles to everyday cars, the world's car manufacturers continue to innovate and improve their products.

Learn more about the European brands, the history of automobiles, or explore Wikipedia's automotive section for more information.

What You'll Find Here

This website is dedicated to exploring the fascinating world of automobiles. From the earliest steam-powered vehicles to modern electric cars, the automotive industry has undergone incredible transformations. Our site covers:

  • History - Trace the evolution of automobiles from 1769 to today
    • Steam engines era
      • 1769 - Nicolas-Joseph Cugnot's steam tricycle
      • 1801 - Richard Trevithick's steam-powered vehicle
    • Internal combustion engines
      • 1876 - Nikolaus Otto's four-stroke engine
      • 1885-1886 - Karl Benz and Gottlieb Daimler's automobiles
  • Brands - Discover famous manufacturers and their contributions
  • Electric Cars - Learn about the sustainable future of transportation
  • Technology - Understand modern automotive innovations
